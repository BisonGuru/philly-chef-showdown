The Neighborhoods Pick
the Finalists.

Three exclusive qualifying tastings roll through North Philly, Old City, and West Philly. Community members taste, vote, and decide who advances. Then 15-20 finalists bring their best to 2300 Arena for the grand finale — celebrity judges, audience choice voting, $10,000 on the line.

15-20 Finalists

Selected through the Qualifying Tasting Tour. The neighborhoods picked them — now they compete for the title.

$10,000 Grand Prize

Cash on the line at 2300 Arena — plus business coaching through MYB's Fractional Executive Program.

Celebrity Judges & Crowd

Pro panel scoring meets live audience-choice voting. Every vote counts in front of 200-500 attendees.

All Philly, All Day

Every chef, every vendor, every performer — born and based in the City of Brotherly Love and Sisterly Affection.

How It Works.

Four steps from your kitchen to the 2300 Arena main stage.

1

Apply

Submit your application online. Tell us who you are, what you cook, and why Philly needs to taste your food.

2

Qualify

Selected applicants are invited to one of three exclusive Tasting Tour stops. Cook your signature dish and earn your spot.

3

Compete

15-20 finalists go head-to-head at 2300 Arena before celebrity judges and a live Philadelphia audience.

4

Win

$10,000 grand prize, business coaching through MYB, and the title of Philly's next great food entrepreneur.

Who Can Compete.

The Philly Chef Showdown is designed for real food businesses with real roots in Philadelphia's neighborhoods. Competitors must meet all four:

✓

Commercial Activity License

Hold a valid City of Philadelphia Commercial Activity License (or have one pending).

✓

Tax Compliant

Current on all City of Philadelphia taxes. Required to compete.

✓

Real Food Business

You're a chef, food truck operator, caterer, or small business restaurant owner.

✓

Neighborhood Roots

Based in or primarily serving an underserved Philadelphia neighborhood.

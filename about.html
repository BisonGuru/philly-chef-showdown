PHILLY
the showdown

The Neighborhoods Pick
the Finalists.

The Philly Chef Showdown is a competition. Three exclusive qualifying tastings roll through Philadelphia's neighborhoods. We'll hit Old City, North and West Philly where community members taste, vote, and decide who advances. The neighborhoods pick the finalists. The neighborhoods pick the categories. It's all about what you want to taste.

The 15 to 20 finalists chosen through the Qualifying Tasting Tour bring their best to 2300 Arena for the grand finale to serve up their best to our panel of celebrity judges. There will also be audience choice voting. A $10,000 grand prize is on the line.

This is Philadelphia's culinary talent at its highest level. Every chef, every vendor and every performer are from the City of Brotherly Love and Sisterly Affection. All Philly, all day.

HOW IT WORKS
from the neighborhood to the main stage

How It Works.

Four steps from your kitchen to the 2300 Arena main stage.

1

Apply

Submit your application online. Tell us who you are, what you cook, and why Philly needs to taste your food.

2

Qualify

Selected applicants are invited to one of three exclusive Tasting Tour stops. Cook your signature dish and earn your spot at the finale.

3

Compete

15-20 finalists compete at 2300 Arena before celebrity judges and a live Philadelphia audience in October.

4

Win

$10,000 grand prize, business coaching through MYB's Fractional Executive Program, and the title.

Capital, capacity, connections.
